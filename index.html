What We Do

Open-source education for engineers

From digital logic to chip verification — structured, community-driven learning resources, free for everyone.

🔬

RTL Design & Verification

Deep-dive guides on writing synthesisable RTL, testbenches, assertions, formal methods, coverage, and simulation using SystemVerilog.

🧰

Toolchain Mastery

Practical references for the tools engineers use every day — Bash, Git, Make, VIM, Python, Linux/Unix, Vivado, and RISC-V GNU toolchain.

📡

Protocols & Interfaces

Structured knowledge on AXI, APB, ACE, CHI, PCIe, DDR, I2C, SPI, UART, USB, and OBI protocol standards.

🌐

Fully Open-Source

All content lives on GitHub. Anyone can read, contribute, open issues, or suggest improvements — no paywalls, no accounts required.
