Story

From Academic Foundation to SoC Leadership

Foundation

He later completed a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology. University became a workshop for experimentation, where theory was tested against practical problems and curiosity turned into disciplined engineering work.

That habit of learning by building still defines his approach: start with fundamentals, work through hard constraints, and keep pushing until the design is robust enough to trust.

Professional Growth

In 2022, he joined Dynamic Solution Innovators as an RTL Design & Verification Engineer and built deep familiarity with AMBA interconnect protocols and verification-heavy frontend work. The early years were defined by rigorous technical growth and careful execution on complex digital systems.

He later led development and verification of an RV64G-based SoC, focusing not only on delivery but also on team culture, collaboration, and sustainable engineering momentum.
