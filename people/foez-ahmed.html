About

In His Own Words

Born in Sylhet, Bangladesh — where modern innovation coexists with historic heritage — Foez has always been drawn to the invisible forces that shape our world. Computer architecture, digital technology, and electronics were never just academic subjects; they were the things that stoked his imagination.

After graduating in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from SUST, he joined DSi in 2022 as an RTL Design & Verification Engineer, diving deep into AMBA system interconnect protocols. He later led a team building and verifying a RISC-V-based SoC, creating a culture of cooperation where creativity could flourish.

Outside of engineering, Foez is a firm believer that teaching is the best way to learn — which is why he built Squared Studio and SkillVerse: to share knowledge, mentor others, and democratise access to high-quality hardware education. He is also an avid gamer, using it to decompress and approach problems from new perspectives.

His ambitions are both ambitious and personal: to consistently advance professionally and spiritually, and to leave a legacy of kindness, wisdom, and inspiration.

Significant Projects

Project Experience

Project 01

RV64GV-based Quad-Core SoC

Contributed to the design and verification of a cutting-edge Quad-Core SoC based on the RV64GV architecture, featuring a dedicated wireless communication subsystem and a shared L2 cache — for a fabless semiconductor company from California.

  • Led architectural definition: core configuration, interconnect strategy, shared L2 cache structure.
  • Established system-level verification methodology with assembly and C tests for multi-core validation.
  • Coordinated design & verification efforts and code reviews across the team.
  • Managed the Git repository for version control and project integration.
Project 02

AXI4 Verification IP

Comprehensive AMBA AXI4 VIP featuring dual master/slave capability, maximum throughput, all burst modes, exclusive access, and QoS reordering — for a fabless semiconductor company from California.

  • Designed and verified a comprehensive AXI4 Master & Slave VIP with exhaustive protocol compliance checks.
  • Implemented all burst modes, exclusive access, and QoS reordering for rigorous transaction verification.
  • Developed a simplified programming interface, reducing test development time.
Project 03

Custom Hardware Coherent Caching System

Customer-specific cache system with private caches interconnected via a 'write update' methodology for automatic data synchronisation — for a fabless semiconductor company from California.

  • Designed and implemented RTL for a custom caching system with write-update coherency.
  • Developed the verification strategy and environment, validating cache coherence across the system.
  • Translated complex requirements into a detailed microarchitecture design and implementation plan.
