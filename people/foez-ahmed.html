Personal Story

The Journey

Born in Bangladesh's dynamic metropolis of Sylhet, Foez developed an early fascination with the invisible systems that shape the modern world. Computer architecture, digital technology, and electronics were never just academic subjects. They were the ideas that kept pulling him toward deeper technical work.

He completed a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, where theory quickly turned into practice. That period built the habit of stepping directly into difficult problems rather than orbiting around them.

In 2022, he started his professional career in RTL design and verification, building a strong base in AMBA system interconnect protocols and high-discipline verification work. That foundation created the confidence to navigate increasingly complex technical programs.

His growth accelerated through leadership on an RV64G-based SoC effort, where the responsibility extended beyond execution into coordination, technical direction, and creating an environment where good engineering decisions could scale across a team.

In January 2026, he joined ADN Semiconductor as a Senior Engineer in RTL Design & Verification, continuing to push deeper into advanced front-end design and meaningful silicon delivery.

Outside client and product work, teaching remains central to how he learns. Sharing knowledge sharpens understanding, and mentoring is treated as part of engineering practice rather than a separate activity.

His long-term aim is straightforward: continue growing technically and personally while leaving behind work that is useful, rigorous, and genuinely helpful to other engineers.
